Part 04 · Innovation · Innovation Builder (Deloitte Ten Types), Audience 3, Grey Nomad Caravan Buyers

Ten innovation dimensions for Coromal and Windsor: from squeezed-middle product brands into managed-lifetime adventure relationships that respect the equity-funded reality of the purchase.

Profit Model
The Built For Life Membership MT Probable
Bundled lifetime membership at point of purchase: extended structural warranty, priority service, mid-trip recovery, annual valuation and Fleetwood-managed resale access. Converts one-off sale into a recurring relationship.
The Guaranteed Buyback Floor MT Probable
Explicit minimum buyback floor at year 7 and year 10, computed against an agreed depreciation curve. Converts resale anxiety (a top forum complaint) into a brand promise Jayco and imports do not match.
Network
The Accredited Dealer Network Program MT Probable
Tiered Accredited Dealer program with training, service-bay standards, customer-data protocols and warranty-handling SLAs. Makes the dealer experience a brand-controlled asset, directly addressing Customer Problem 7.
The Grey Nomad Community Partnership ST Probable
Partnerships with The Grey Nomads, Caravanning with Kids and major grey-nomad YouTube channels for content, owner stories and structured reviewer access. Rebalances brand presence in the forums Audience 3 actually uses.
Product Performance
The Trip-Typed Power System Range MT Probable
Battery, solar, DC-DC and inverter spec matched to four named trip archetypes (coastal touring, snowbird migration, outback expedition, family weekend). Converts spec-sheet confusion into a guided decision addressing Customer Problem 6.
The Easy-Tow Chassis Variant MT Probable
Active sway control, lower hitch weight, integrated reversing assistance and improved aerodynamic profile for ageing buyers. Addresses cognitive-load and reflex concerns that older buyers rarely articulate but consistently act on.
Product System
The Off-Grid Touring Bundle MT Probable
Lithium battery, solar, satellite communication, water-recycling and composting-toilet option configured as a single product system. Simplifies the off-grid decision for buyers who want capability without complexity.
The Connected Caravan Telematics System MT Probable
Battery state, water levels, tyre pressure, GPS theft alert and trip log accessible via a Coromal-Windsor app. Supports the lifetime warranty process and trip-typology service.
Service
The 24/7 On-Road Support Service MT Probable
24/7 on-road technical support: telephone support, dispatched mobile technicians in major regional hubs and follow-up coordination. Operationalises Brand Opportunity 3 and directly addresses Customer Problem 7.
The Annual Pre-Trip Service ST Probable
Standardised annual pre-trip service at accredited dealers covering chassis, electrical, gas, brakes, water systems and exterior seals. Converts service from a reactive event into an ownership ritual.
Brand & Customer Engagement
The Built For Life Brand Promise MT Probable
Coromal-Windsor relaunch under a unified Built For Life promise: 20-year structural warranty, buyback floor, dealer network and trip-typology service. Supported by a long-form Australian manufacturing provenance campaign.
The Coromal-Windsor Owners Club ST Probable
Formalised owners club with regional chapters, annual gatherings, branded merchandise and member benefits. Jayco operates this well; Coromal-Windsor currently under-invest in a proven loyalty asset.
Audience logic
This audience consumes caravans as identity vehicles for self-authored retirement. The unifying logic: convert Coromal and Windsor from squeezed-middle product brands into managed-lifetime adventure relationships that respect the equity-funded reality of the purchase. ST = 0-6 months, MT = 6-12 months, LT = 12+ months. Probable
