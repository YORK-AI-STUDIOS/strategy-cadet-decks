The Modular Voice Vacuum
Fleetwood's revenue share (~14-18% estimated) materially exceeds its estimated SoS (~3.5%). The brand is radically under-voicing relative to its operational scale. Ausco, Hickory and Modscape are all voicing above their revenue share. This is the single largest brand opportunity in the category. Estimated
ESOV classification: Defense Mode
Fleetwood's ESOV position is Defense Mode: holding revenue share without corresponding voice share. The ESOV gap creates headroom for aggressive voice investment without competitors having material response time. Competitors are in Expansion Mode or Bid Room Mode, not positioned to match a step-change in Fleetwood thought-leadership output. Estimated
RV category: Under-Indexed
Fleetwood's RV Solutions segment carries zero independent SoS in current caravan-brand category queries (see GEO Scorecard p.57). Coromal and Windsor now sit inside Apollo's brand universe for search purposes. The RV segment's SoS is effectively nil from a Fleetwood corporate brand standpoint. Estimated
KPI target (Le6)
KPI Le6: +200 basis points SoS over baseline by Day 180. On the estimated ~3.5% baseline, +200 bps = approximately 5.5% target. Given the Voice Vacuum, this is achievable with consistent trade-press thought leadership, case-study publication, and conference presence. The constraint is editorial discipline, not media spend.
