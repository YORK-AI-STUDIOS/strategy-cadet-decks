The Modular Voice Vacuum
No AU modular incumbent is voicing the category at scale. Modscape and Hickory are claiming category leadership on editorial credit alone, while Ausco defends via service and scale without above-the-line presence. Fleetwood, despite its 60-year history and ASX listing, is currently not in that race. The first credible category-leadership voice wins a disproportionate share-of-mind premium. This is the central ESOV opportunity. Probable
