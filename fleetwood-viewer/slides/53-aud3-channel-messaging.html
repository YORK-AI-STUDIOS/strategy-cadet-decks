Aged 55-75, couple-based purchase, owner-occupier, household drawdown AUD 90K-300K, capital-city outer suburbs and coastal sea-change postcodes

Top CEPs
Retirement Date Set (life-stage), Big Lap Bucket-List Trigger (identity)
Strategist flag: The Audience 3 plan rests on the structural assumption that the RV segment is worth defending rather than divesting. Section 6 classifies the RV side as Under-Indexed and FY25 saw approximately 6% revenue contraction; the squeezed-middle position is the most vulnerable in the bifurcating Australian RV category. The plan budgets 22% on dealer network capability investment, a significant capex commitment to a declining segment. If the client's strategic intent is to harvest rather than defend RV Solutions, the Audience 3 plan needs to be rebriefed before any tactic is committed. Hypothesised

Messaging by Funnel Stage
Awareness
"The Big Lap belongs to Australians who built it. Coromal and Windsor have been built in Australia for sixty years, for the road conditions Australians actually drive."
Pillar: Made To Outlast plus Sovereign-Capable Modular. Proof: 60-year Fleetwood heritage, Australian factory provenance. CEPs: Retirement Date Set, Big Lap Bucket-List Trigger.
Consideration
"Towing dynamics, off-grid systems and chassis design are not optional features. Every Coromal and Windsor ships with trip-typology-matched power and a chassis spec audited for the corrugations of the Tanami and the Gibb."
Pillar: Made To Outlast. Proof: Trip-Typed Power System Range, Easy-Tow Chassis Variant. CEPs: Big Lap Bucket-List Trigger, Coastal Snowbird Migration, First-Van Upgrade.
Conversion
"Buy the van and the lifetime around it. Built For Life membership bundles 20-year structural warranty, accredited dealer service nationwide, on-road recovery and a published resale-value floor."
Pillars: Managed Adventure plus Made To Outlast. Proof: The Built For Life Membership, Guaranteed Buyback Floor, Accredited Dealer Network. CEPs: Equity Release Decision, Retirement Date Set.
Loyalty
"Wherever you are when something happens, we are there. The Coromal-Windsor on-road recovery network covers every sealed-road postcode in Australia, and the Lifetime Ownership Office answers."
Pillar: Managed Adventure. Proof: 24/7 On-Road Support Service, Lifetime Ownership Office. CEPs: Health-Wake-Up Moment, Grandchildren in Tow.
