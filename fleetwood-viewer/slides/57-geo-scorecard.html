The "Recall Without Recommendation" Trap
AI assistants know who Fleetwood is when asked, but never volunteer Fleetwood when asked who is good. The brand's AI footprint is defensive, not generative. Ausco, Modscape, Crusader, and Jayco own the unprompted answer slots. This is the single most actionable insight from the scan. Verified
