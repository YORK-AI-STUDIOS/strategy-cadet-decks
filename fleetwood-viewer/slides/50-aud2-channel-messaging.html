Messaging by Funnel Stage
Awareness
"Australia's housing shortage and education backlog are not solved with traditional construction. They are solved with classrooms, clinics and social-housing blocks that open before the next election."
Pillar: Reliability Beyond Doubt. Proof: Fleetwood school classroom deployments, FY25 +20.3% revenue growth. CEPs: Election-Cycle Ribbon Cut, Housing Supply Target Push.
Consideration
"Every Fleetwood building ships with documented embodied carbon, NCC 2025 compliance evidence, and an Australian Industry Capability plan auditable down to component level."
Pillar: Disclosed By Default. Proof: Modular Carbon Ledger (BO-10), 90-Day Embodied-Carbon Audit, AASB S2 disclosure pack. CEPs: Net-Zero Estate Refresh, Panel Refresh Tender.
Conversion
"Fleetwood is panel-listed across the eight largest Australian public-works pathways. Specify once, deploy anywhere, with whole-of-life pricing transparent at signature."
Pillars: Reliability Beyond Doubt plus Made To Outlast. Proof: Multi-State Panel Stack, Whole-Of-Life Pricing Bundle, 30-Year Asset Management Service. CEPs: Panel Refresh Tender, Workforce Housing Tied to Service Delivery.
Loyalty
"When the school is replaced or the village is decommissioned, the modules redeploy into social housing or Indigenous community housing under a managed pathway, with the carbon credit transferred to the next operator."
Pillar: Circular By Construction. Proof: Decommission-To-Social-Housing Service, Modular Carbon Ledger (BO-10). CEPs: Net-Zero Estate Refresh, Remote School or Clinic Build.
