Sincerity and competence dominant. Excitement and sophistication the strategic gaps.

Fleetwood (assessed)
Category average (AU industrial + RV, estimated)
Hypothesised
Sincerity
Honest, wholesome, genuine. Fleetwood's sixty-year track record and refusal to overclaim earn a high sincerity rating. The foreman voice is structurally sincere. Hypothesised
Competence
Reliable, responsible, expert. Engineering-led communication and a B2B procurement focus produce strong competence signals. Tender-win record and Searipple Village operationalise it. Hypothesised
Ruggedness
Outdoorsy, tough, Australian conditions. Both a Pilbara workforce village and a Coromal caravan on the Tanami must survive corrugations and heat. Ruggedness is structural to both product lines. Probable
Excitement
Daring, spirited, imaginative. Low by design. The brand does not behave like a consumer brand even on the consumer side. This is the gap that under-serves capital markets and younger talent. Hypothesised
Sophistication
Upper-class, charming, glamorous. Very low. Fleetwood deliberately avoids glamour positioning. Correct for B2B and resources procurement. A risk on consumer caravans versus Jayco's aspirational styling. Hypothesised
