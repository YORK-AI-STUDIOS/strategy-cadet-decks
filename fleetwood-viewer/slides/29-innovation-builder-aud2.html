Part 04 · Innovation · Innovation Builder (Deloitte Ten Types), Audience 2, Public-Sector Modular

Ten innovation dimensions applied to public-sector modular: Fleetwood converts from modular vendor into a policy-delivery partner delivering ribbon-cuttable buildings inside a political cycle.

Profit Model
The Whole-Of-Life Pricing Bundle MT Probable
Schools and social housing priced on a 30-year whole-of-life basis (operating performance, embodied and operational carbon), favouring Fleetwood's factory-controlled quality over lower-capex competitors with worse whole-of-life economics.
The Pre-Event Readiness Subscription LT Hypothesised
State emergency-management agencies subscribe to a Fleetwood readiness reserve (classrooms, clinics, accommodation) activating inside 14 days of a declared disaster. Converts post-event tender procurement into a pre-event subscription model.
Network
The Multi-State Panel Stack MT Probable
Active panel listings on Local Buy (QLD), NSW Government Procurement, Buying for Victoria, WA Common Use Arrangements and equivalent SA, NT, TAS plus Commonwealth arrangements. Converts panel listings into portable distribution.
The Net-Zero Alliance Network MT Probable
Fleetwood convenes a net-zero alliance with mass-timber producers, low-carbon concrete suppliers, embodied-carbon assurance providers and recycled-content partners, packaged as a delivery alliance for state net-zero estate-refresh programs.
Product Performance
The Zero-Carbon Classroom MT Probable
Modular classroom rated to zero-operational-carbon threshold, with documented embodied-carbon disclosure, mass-timber lining and integrated solar-plus-battery. Purpose-built for state Education department net-zero estate-refresh tenders.
The 50-Unit Social Housing Block MT Probable
Standardised 50-unit modular social-housing block for Housing Accord-aligned delivery, with universal-access compliance and a 30-year operational-cost profile. Simplifies state housing authority procurement.
Product System
The Modular School Campus System MT Probable
Complete modular school campus (classrooms, library, admin, hall, integrated energy and connectivity) as a single product. Allows state Education departments to specify one product rather than a tender list. Unlocks ribbon-cut-inside-political-cycle delivery.
The Remote Clinic Module MT Probable
Remote-clinic modular variant for Aboriginal Community Controlled Health Organisations and rural primary-health-network deployment, with telehealth-ready connectivity and clinical-grade fitouts. Unlocks the Remote-Service Equity demand cell.
Process
The Probity-Pack Standard Operating Procedure ST Probable
Published probity-pack SOP for every bid, containing compliance evidence, references, embodied-carbon disclosures, Indigenous-content statements and AIC-plan extracts. Reduces bid effort and standardises the probity story.
The Embodied-Carbon Evidence Pipeline MT Probable
Continuous embodied-carbon evidence pipeline producing project-level disclosures aligned with NCC 2025 and AASB S2 requirements, ready to attach to any state estate-refresh bid. Turns disclosure obligation into a marketing engine.
Brand & Customer Engagement
The Disclosed-By-Default Brand ST Probable
Fleetwood publishes full AASB S2 disclosure, transition plan and embodied-carbon methodology ahead of the FY26 deadline, using the disclosure as a brand launch moment. Converts a compliance milestone into category leadership.
The Public-Sector Modular Leaders Roundtable ST Probable
Quarterly leaders roundtable across state and federal public-sector modular procurement leaders, hosted as a peer forum on housing, education and remote-service delivery. Creates a brand-owned policy conversation.
Audience logic
This audience procures modular schools, social housing, clinics and emergency accommodation. The unifying logic: Fleetwood converts from a modular vendor into a policy-delivery partner whose product is the ribbon-cuttable building inside a political cycle. ST = 0-6 months, MT = 6-12 months, LT = 12+ months. Probable
