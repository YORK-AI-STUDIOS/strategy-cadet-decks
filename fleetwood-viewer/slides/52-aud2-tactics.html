Part 07 · Marketing Plans · Audience 2: Public-Sector Modular, Campaign Tactics and Creative Direction

Five named campaign directions. The Disclosed By Default Launch is calendar-forced by the September 2026 deadline and is the highest-stakes single play. The Ribbon-Cut Festival is the easiest to start now.

The Politically Investable Building Programme
Complexity: Medium Public-sector press LinkedIn Ministerial briefings
Long-form editorial and case-study programme positioning Fleetwood as the vendor that ships buildings inside one political cycle. Anchored by three named lighthouse projects (one school, one social-housing block, one remote clinic) with verifiable opening dates.
Why this audience: Directly activates the Election-Cycle Ribbon Cut and Housing Supply Target Push CEPs and the "Speed-to-Occupancy Expectation" signal. Innovation sources: Policy Delivery Partner Brand, 50-Unit Social Housing Block, Zero-Carbon Classroom.
Disclosed By Default Launch (September 2026 FY26 Annual Report)
Complexity: High Trade press LinkedIn Disclosure pack Policy engagement
Treats the mandatory AASB S2 disclosure as a brand launch rather than a regulatory filing. Ships the disclosure pack alongside an open-source embodied-carbon toolkit and a Modular Carbon Ledger demo. Calendar-forced by the September 2026 Annual Report drop.
Why this audience: Converts Driver 7 (AASB S2) from a barrier into a positioning advantage. Activates "Sustainability and Low-Carbon Delivery" signal and Trend B1 "Probity-First Brand Building". Innovation sources: Disclosed-By-Default Brand, Open Source Embodied-Carbon Toolkit.
The Multi-State Panel Stack Roadshow
Complexity: Medium Industry-association Policy media Panel platforms
Six-city briefing roadshow timed to state-panel refresh windows (NSW, VIC, QLD, WA, SA, plus Commonwealth), delivering the Fleetwood probity pack, panel submission template and case-study library to state procurement leads.
Why this audience: Direct access to the Panel Refresh Tender CEP and "Digital-First Procurement Culture" signal. Innovation sources: Multi-State Panel Stack (Network), Probity-Pack Standard Operating Procedure (Process).
The Open Modular Standard
Complexity: High Industry-association Open-source toolkit Policy media
Fleetwood convenes a Public-Sector Modular Leaders Roundtable and publishes a draft Modern Methods of Construction type-approval pathway, donating the work to the National Construction Code Board.
Why this audience: Activates "Housing-First Social Urgency" signal and BO-5. Positions Fleetwood as convenor not vendor, the highest credibility tier in public-sector probity logic. Innovation sources: MMC Type-Approval Process, Net-Zero Alliance Network.
Ribbon-Cut Festival
Complexity: Low (per cluster) Public-sector press Ministerial engagement LinkedIn
Coordinated public-event calendar where Fleetwood-built schools, clinics and social-housing blocks open in clusters of three to five per quarter, with state ministers and federal Housing Australia leads invited. Each cluster generates a content asset and a Housing Accord public update.
Why this audience: Activates the Election-Cycle Ribbon Cut CEP directly and converts delivery cadence into earned-media cadence. Innovation sources: The Ribbon-Cut Festival, The Housing Accord Public Update (Customer Engagement).
Creative Direction (top 3 Primary Channels)
Public-sector trade press and policy media
Institutional but warm: named teachers, nurses and tenants in completed Fleetwood facilities rather than empty render glamour. Hook: "the politically investable building", grounded in the Election-Cycle Ribbon Cut CEP. Format: 1,000-1,500 word op-ed under Head of Public Sector byline, quarterly long-form features in The Mandarin and Government News.
LinkedIn organic and paid, public-sector targeted
Documentary-quiet, leaning into the "personality of the foreman" but updated for the public-sector reader: factory-floor footage, BIM model walkthroughs, AIC plan extracts on screen. Hook: factory line economics are the public-sector procurement story. Format: 1,200 word LinkedIn article, NCC 2025 explainer carousels, 30-60 second BIM clips, monthly Sales Navigator Lead Gen Form sequences.
Industry-association events and panels
Research-led, with one signature visual asset: a comparative timeline of site-built versus Fleetwood modular delivery against a state election cycle. Hook: modular as policy-delivery infrastructure. Format: 25-minute keynote, co-authored panel paper with Property Council or Master Builders, roundtable working session producing a named output at the close.
