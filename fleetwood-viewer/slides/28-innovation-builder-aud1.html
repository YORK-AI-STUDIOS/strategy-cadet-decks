Part 04 · Innovation · Innovation Builder (Deloitte Ten Types), Audience 1, Resources & Defence

Ten innovation dimensions applied to workforce accommodation: Fleetwood converts from modular vendor into underwriter of workforce-outcome contracts.

Profit Model
The Bednight Annuity MT Probable
Per-occupied-bednight pricing on multi-year commitment. Converts village revenue to annuity income aligned with operator retention outcomes.
The Schedule-Default Credit Mechanism MT Probable
Every modular delivery contract includes a published credit converting delay days to financial credit on the next phase. Implements the Scheduled-Outcome Contract.
Network
The Defence Prime Tier-One Stack MT Probable
Formalise tier-one supplier relationships with BAE Systems AU, Austal and Civmec, joining AUKUS pillar 1 and Defence Strategic Review delivery teams at Henderson, Tindal and Garden Island.
The Indigenous Business Partnership Network ST Probable
Joint-venture relationships with Indigenous-owned businesses in Pilbara, Kimberley and NT, converting ESG compliance into a partnership growth lever.
Product Performance
The Acoustic-Certified Sleeper Module MT Probable
Sleeper module rated to defined acoustic-separation threshold (Rw 55), third-party certified, carrying a price premium for the psychosocial-safety-sensitive market.
The Connected Module Telemetry Layer MT Probable
Embedded telemetry covering energy, water, occupancy and HVAC, integrated into a Fleetwood API platform. Manifests the Modular Carbon Ledger opportunity.
Product System
The Village-As-Platform System MT Probable
Complete village as a configured platform: sleeper modules, amenity, dining, allied-health and connectivity backbone with standardised interfaces. Underwrites the Scheduled-Outcome Contract.
The Searipple Platform Reference Architecture ST Probable
Searipple Village configuration published as a reference architecture customers can specify into RFPs, creating a Fleetwood-defined category standard.
Service
The Wellbeing Programming Service MT Probable
Mental-health first aid, fitness, group-activity coordination and off-rotation transition support bundled into village contracts as a billable service layer.
Brand & Customer Engagement
The Workforce Wellbeing Convenor Position MT Probable
Fleetwood publishes the Wellbeing Specification, hosts an annual industry summit, positions itself as the category convenor for workforce wellbeing in remote Australia.
The Project Director Roundtable ST Probable
Quarterly roundtable across Perth, Brisbane and Darwin, hosted at executive level, covering workforce-accommodation challenges. A relationship forum no incumbent currently runs.
Audience logic
This audience consumes accommodation as workforce-retention infrastructure inside schedule-critical resources and defence projects. The unifying logic: Fleetwood converts from a vendor of modular units into an underwriter of workforce-outcome contracts. ST = 0-6 months, MT = 6-12 months, LT = 12+ months. Probable
