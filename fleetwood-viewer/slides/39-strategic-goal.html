Recommended Strategic Goal
Expand into Australian public-sector and resources modular category leadership via a packaged "Scheduled-Outcome Contract plus Workforce Wellbeing Specification" offer over 180 days.
Sanity check: PASS WITH NOTES Probable

Pass Sanity Check 1, Is "AU public-sector and resources modular" a meaningful category?
AUD 14.44B Australian prefabricated buildings market, growing 6.2% CAGR to 2034. Fleetwood named by Mordor Intelligence as a major operating company alongside Ausco, Modscape, Hickory and Archiblox. Open white-space at the voice layer: Ausco is silent, Hickory and Modscape claim via editorial, Decmil is in bid-room mode. Fleetwood at ~3.5% estimated share has room to expand voice and share simultaneously. Verified [40][2]
Pass Sanity Check 2, Is the implicit audience supported by the discovery scope?
Targets Audience 1 (Resources and Defence, 40-60) and Audience 2 (Public-Sector Modular, 35-55), both richly profiled with Cultural Signals, Purchase Drivers and CEPs. Dominant shared driver: reduced friction and de-risked schedule. Both score 5/5 on Product Quality, 5/5 on Customer Service, 4/5 on Innovation. Scheduled-Outcome Contract pitch lands on schedule-defence driver in both; Wellbeing Specification lands on Trust and Impact (5/5 in both). Probable
Pass Sanity Check 3, Is the mechanism supported by the ideation pool?
Maps to Brand Opportunities 1 and 2 directly (both PROBABLE). Reinforced by Emerging Trends A1 (Outcome-Defended Contracting) and C2 (Wellbeing-Integrated Module). Does not require new product development: productisation of capabilities Fleetwood already operates. Searipple Village (1,300 rooms, FY25 named earnings uplift driver) provides the operating laboratory. No mechanism gap flagged. Probable
Why this goal, not another
Three forces line up: (1) Federal 2025-26 Budget AUD 54M direct + AUD 120M via National Productivity Fund for modular uptake. (2) "The Modular Voice Vacuum", no incumbent voicing the category at scale. (3) Agents C and D converge on a single high-conviction value proposition that maps directly onto Audience 1 and 2 purchase drivers scoring 5/5. The 180-day horizon is chosen so the September 2026 AASB S2 disclosure-shock event lands inside the plan window rather than after it. Probable [105][40]
Framing note
Fleetwood is already a named leader (Mordor Intelligence). This goal does not invent a position, it activates a dormant one. The verb "expand" is honest: Fleetwood is in Defense Mode on modular but has not yet claimed a visible category-leadership voice. The mechanism converts operational capability into a marketed product category. Probable
