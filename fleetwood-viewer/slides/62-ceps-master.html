Part 09 · Appendix · Category Entry Points, All Three Audiences

21 Category Entry Points across three audiences: seven per audience, spanning Life-stage, Occasion, Need-state, Identity, Emotion and Location triggers. These are the moments that open the buying window.

Audience 1, Resources and Defence (7 CEPs)
Life-stage
New Project FID Trigger
"Board just approved final investment decision, I have 18 months to stand up a 1,200-bed village in the Pilbara."
Occasion
Shutdown Surge
"Major maintenance shutdown lands in October, I need 400 extra beds for ten weeks and they have to arrive yesterday."
Need-state
Retention Crisis Response
"Turnover hit 38% last quarter, the village is on the exit-interview list, we have to upgrade amenity before the next contract round."
Identity
ESG Audit Pressure
"Our TCFD disclosures need a credible accommodation-sustainability story, embodied carbon and operational emissions both."
Occasion
Defence Estate Refresh
"Henderson or Tindal needs new permanent accommodation under the Defence Strategic Review, tendering the modular package now."
Emotion
Psychosocial Incident Aftermath
"We had an incident, the regulator is watching, I cannot specify a village that fails a wellbeing audit."
Need-state
Decommission and Redeploy
"Project is winding down, can these units be relocated to the next site or sold into social housing rather than landfilled?"
Audience 2, Public-Sector Modular (7 CEPs)
Occasion
Election-Cycle Ribbon Cut
"Minister promised 50 new classrooms before the next election, we need a modular partner who can deliver inside 18 months."
Identity
Housing Supply Target Push
"State has committed to a National Housing Accord target, my division has to add thousands of social-housing dwellings."
Need-state
Disaster Recovery Mobilisation
"Floods displaced a community, we need temporary classrooms, clinics and worker accommodation on-ground in weeks not months."
Identity
Net-Zero Estate Refresh
"Whole-of-government net-zero plan requires lower-embodied-carbon new builds, modular is the route I can defend at the Auditor-General review."
Location
Remote School or Clinic Build
"Project is six hours from the nearest regional centre, site-built conventional is impossible, modular is the only realistic pathway."
Occasion
Panel Refresh Tender
"State modular-construction panel is up for re-tender, we are short-listing for the next four-year period."
Need-state
Workforce Housing Tied to Service Delivery
"We cannot recruit teachers or nurses to this town without housing, accommodation is now part of the service-delivery brief."
Audience 3, Grey Nomad Caravan Buyers (7 CEPs)
Life-stage
Retirement Date Set
"I have a retirement date on the calendar, the caravan is the next big purchase before then."
Identity
Big Lap Bucket-List Trigger
"We always said we would do the Lap, and we are finally going to do it next winter."
Occasion
Coastal Snowbird Migration
"Melbourne winter is brutal, we want a setup that lets us spend three months north every year."
Identity
Equity Release Decision
"We are downsizing the house, a portion of the equity is earmarked for the caravan and the travel fund."
Life-stage
First-Van Upgrade
"We did the first lap in a budget van, now we know what we want and we are buying properly for the long term."
Emotion
Health-Wake-Up Moment
"One of us had a health scare and we realised we should stop deferring the life we planned and do it now."
Occasion
Grandchildren in Tow
"Our grandkids want to do a trip with us in the school holidays, we need a van that can actually fit them."
