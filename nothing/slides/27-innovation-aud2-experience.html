Verified x1
Brand Innovation
Brand · Probable
Annual Nothing Teardown Report
Short-term
Annual published teardown report co-produced with iFixit. Repairability scoring, spare parts availability, improvement trajectory vs prior year. iFixit co-brand provides third-party credibility. Addresses Sustainability as Identity trend (Aud 2).
Brand · Probable
Carl Pei: Quarterly Australian AMA
Short-term
Quarterly live AMA on the Nothing community forum and YouTube for the Australian community. Carl Pei answers questions submitted by Aud 2 without PR filter. Topic scope: product decisions, spec trade-offs, strategic direction.
Brand · Probable
Nothing Manifesto: Annual State of the Brand
Short-term
Annually published brand document reflecting on product decisions, what Nothing would do differently, and 12-month commitments. No PR polish. Grounded in Disruption-First Mindset trend (Aud 2) and the brand value of Transparency.
Brand · Probable
Hardware Honesty Series: Why We Made This Call
Short-term
Documentary content series where Nothing engineers explain specific hardware decisions in technical detail before major launches. Published on YouTube and community forum. Meets Aud 2 in their spec-interrogation mode with first-party information.
Channel Innovation
Channel · Probable
r/NothingTech Australian Community Liaison
Short-term
Nominated Nothing community liaison participating in r/NothingTech with transparent identity (flagged as Nothing staff). Answers technical questions, escalates feedback to product teams, posts launch-day AMAs. Grounded in Reddit Rabbit Hole Discovery CEP (Aud 2).
Channel · Probable
Mid-Tier YouTube Creator Programme
Short-term
Early-access programme for AU YouTube reviewers in the 20K-200K subscriber tier, 3-4 weeks before launch. No review embargo conditions. Full editorial independence. Mid-tier reviewers carry higher audience trust per view than large channels.
Channel · Probable
JB Hi-Fi In-Store Demo Enhancement
Short-term
Dedicated Nothing demo station at top 10 AU JB Hi-Fi locations. Live Glyph demos, comparison unit, QR code to Hardware Honesty Series. Directly addresses JB Hi-Fi In-Store Encounter CEP (Aud 2) [143].
Channel · Probable
Tech Community Discord Investment
Medium-term
Nothing-operated AU tech Discord with early build notes, beta testing access, and direct product team communication. Transparent moderation policy posted publicly. Community-elected moderator council. Grounded in Authenticity Over Gloss trend (Aud 2).
Customer Engagement + Service Innovation
Engagement · Verified
Beta Build Access: AU Early Adopter Tier
Short-term
VERIFIED: Nothing already operates a global beta programme. This formalises and localises it with Australian community members (100-500 users), structured feedback collection, and a published changelog crediting community contributions by name.
Engagement · Probable
Community Hardware Vote: AU Exclusive Colourway
Medium-term
Annual community vote where AU Aud 2 members select a limited AU-exclusive transparent-back colourway or material variant. Voting open to registered community members only. Community governance of a product decision is itself brand disruption.
Service · Probable
Software Support Commitment: Published Schedule
Short-term
Publicly published software update commitment for all current Nothing devices: last OS update year, security patch end date, quarterly update schedule. Published on nothing.tech/au and pinned in community forum.
Service · Probable
iFixit Certified Repair Partnership
Medium-term
Formal partnership to publish official Nothing repair guides, supply genuine spare parts via iFixit platform, and target iFixit repairability score of 7+ for all future flagship devices. Addresses Sustainability as Identity trend (Aud 2).
