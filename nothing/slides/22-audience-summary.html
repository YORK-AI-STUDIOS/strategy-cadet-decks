Tech-Forward Urban Creatives
Aged 22-34 · Design-Obsessed · AUD 60-110K
Primary trigger

Aesthetic credential. The phone is an identity signal at client meetings and gallery openings. Brand Image (5/5) and Innovation (5/5) lead all other drivers.

Discovery path

Reddit, Dezeen, Instagram. Glyph-first unboxing moment. Anti-Apple statement. Portfolio shoot prop.

Audience 02 of 03
Anti-Mainstream Gadget Upgraders
Aged 25-38 · Challenger-Brand Champions · AUD 85-140K
Primary trigger

Spec trust + founder credibility. Product Quality (5/5) and Trust (5/5) are co-equal top drivers. Any spec regression is publicly amplified.

Discovery path

Reddit r/NothingTech at 11pm. iPhone price increase. JB Hi-Fi in-store transparent-back encounter. Spec-sheet validation.

Loyalty cliff warning

Loyalty is conditional and revocable. Chipset downgrade, spec mislead, or founder over-marketing triggers community amplification in the exact audience most likely to evangelise Nothing. Probable

Audience 03 of 03
Budget-Conscious Gen Z Experimenters
Aged 18-27 · Social-Proof Driven · AUD 25-65K
Primary trigger

Price accessibility via BNPL. Price (5/5) is the primary filter. Discovery on TikTok; BNPL closes the purchase. Social proof outweighs brand advertising at every stage.

Discovery path

TikTok unboxing spiral. Phone contract end point. BNPL accessibility moment (AUD 699 = 4 x AUD 175). Budget phone shame escape.

Key insight

Journey starts on TikTok, ends at checkout via phone. Peer validation closes the sale. BNPL and mid-range positioning makes Nothing attainable. Authenticity is load-bearing.
