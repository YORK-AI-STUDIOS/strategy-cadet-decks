Bimodal Viral Distribution (Strategic Finding)
Nothing's TikTok content does not follow a unimodal distribution. Two distinct performance bands exist: an organic ceiling for standard content, and a separate engineered-outlier band for format-optimised and amplified content. The implication for strategy: the content type matters more than the posting frequency. VERIFIED from 250-post dataset
