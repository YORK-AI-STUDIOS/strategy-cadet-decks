Methodology note · Important limitation
Web-search proxy, not direct AI API query
True GEO measurement requires direct API queries to multiple AI platforms (each requires a paid tier, approximately USD 5 to 20 per month minimum). This Cadet GEO scan uses editorial content indexed by search engines as a proxy for what AI models would retrieve via RAG or training data. Search index results may lag actual AI training data by 6 to 12 months. For brands with API budget, direct prompt-testing across multiple models is the upgrade path. [Honest Limitations section]
