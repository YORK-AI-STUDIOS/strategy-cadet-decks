Founding story (under 50 words)

Carl Pei co-founded Nothing in 2020 after leaving OnePlus, believing consumer tech had lost its ability to excite. Backed by Tony Fadell and Casey Neistat, Nothing set out to build a world where technology is fun and relevant again, starting with audio before moving to phones. Probable

Mission (publicly stated)

"We exist to make technology fun and relevant again." Verified

Why founder trust matters

Carl Pei's public AMAs, product candour, and blunt communications style are a structural trust asset that no Samsung or Apple campaign equivalent can replicate. The founder persona is load-bearing for Audience 2 (Anti-Mainstream Gadget Upgraders). Probable

Company timeline
2020
Nothing Technology founded. Carl Pei leaves OnePlus. Backed by Tony Fadell (iPod inventor), Casey Neistat, Steve Huffman. London HQ. [3]
2021
Ear (1) launched. Nothing's first product. Transparent stem earbuds. Establishes the transparent-design language before phones enter the picture.
2022
Phone (1) launched. First Nothing smartphone. Glyph Interface debut. Cult-following established in UK, India, and enthusiast communities globally.
2023
Phone (2) and CMF sub-brand launched. AU market entry via JB Hi-Fi. [143] USD 500M lifetime sales milestone. CMF extends the brand into the budget tier.
2024
Phone (2a) series. USD 500M+ annual revenue; 7M+ devices; first profitable year. India: 577% YoY growth. [2] [227]
2025
Phone (3), Headphone (1), USD 200M Series C. Tiger Global lead; USD 1.3B valuation; Qualcomm Ventures joins. AI-first device confirmed for 2026. [159] [225]
2026
Charli XCX named Global Brand Ambassador and investor. Phone (4a) series AU launch. Optus carrier partnership. [251] [265]
