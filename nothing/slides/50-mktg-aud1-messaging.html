Funnel Stage Core Message CEPs addressed
Awareness Nothing makes the decision to show its work: every component, every circuit, every LED is an argument that hardware confidence doesn't need to be hidden. For design-literate Australians, this is a phone with a position, not just a specification. Glyph-First Unboxing Moment (Identity). Anti-Apple Statement Upgrade (Emotion).
Consideration The Glyph Interface is the only ambient notification layer in mass-market consumer electronics: it communicates without demanding attention, and that changes how a creative professional manages focus across a working day. Creative Tool Ecosystem Lock-In (Need-State). Content Creation Companion (Occasion).
Conversion At AUD 599-849, Nothing positions itself as the design object that doesn't compromise the aesthetic of your working life, and ships with above-average software support so the decision pays forward across three to four years. Earbuds-Before-Phone Discovery (Life-stage). Portfolio Shoot Prop Upgrade (Occasion).
Loyalty Nothing updates its software in the same voice it ships its hardware: direct, annotated, and honest about what changed and why. For creative professionals who treat every tool as a long-term relationship, this is a brand that earns the right to stay on your desk. Design Event Social Proof (Social).
