What this means
ROLLiN’ and Bingle share the largest gap. Both are price-led digital challengers. The same product economics produce the same service economics.

NRMA closes the gap to half a star because its post-claim service is the category benchmark. The 2.4-star ROLLiN’ gap is not a measurement artefact, it is a structural disclosure.
