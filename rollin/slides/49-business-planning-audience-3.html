Plan read

For this cohort the plan is built around uptime and cash flow. Every concept defends the car as an income asset: a no-denial pledge, income-aligned billing, downtime measured in hours, a climate income rider. Channels run through gig communities and platform APIs, not consumer media. The metrics are operational, not brand.
