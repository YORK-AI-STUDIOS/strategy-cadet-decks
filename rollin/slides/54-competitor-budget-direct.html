Competitor 02

Budget Direct The value challenger that balances low cost with comprehensive cover. Australia’s most-awarded insurer.

Auto & General / BHL Group
Verified
The brand

The perennial value-award winner, with a 19-year streak to defend.

Budget Direct is underwritten by Auto and General Insurance Company Limited, part of the BHL Group. It is Australia’s most awarded insurer by Canstar, holding 19 consecutive national Outstanding Value awards as of 2025.

The brand is positioned as the value challenger that balances low cost with comprehensive cover, and it backs that position with weight: it ranked 17th in Nielsen’s top Australian advertisers in 2025.

Why it matters to ROLLiN’

Budget Direct is a key volume competitor across all demographics. Its scale and award equity make it the default value choice on comparison sites, the same discovery channel ROLLiN’s younger audiences use.
