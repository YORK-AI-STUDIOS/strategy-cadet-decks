Brand DNA · Product & uniqueness
Probable
Core product

Comprehensive car insurance, sold and serviced through the app.

ROLLiN’ offers comprehensive car insurance to Australian drivers, sold and serviced almost entirely through a mobile-led digital experience. Cover is positioned as a single, simplified comprehensive product rather than a sprawling matrix of optional add-ons.

  • Quoting, binding, mid-term changes, and claims all designed to run through the app or web with minimal call-centre touch.
  • Pricing is usage-aware, tilted toward drivers who do lower kilometres or who can demonstrate safer driving behaviour.
  • IAG underwriting and claims infrastructure behind it. Challenger front end, incumbent-grade balance sheet.
What makes it unique

Three reasons combined, not one in isolation.

  • True digital-native challenger backed by the country’s largest general insurer. Moves on customer experience without the capital and reinsurance risk a true startup carries.
  • Deliberately narrow product. One core comprehensive policy, fewer levers, less confusion. Stripped-back is treated as a feature.
  • Openly anti-jargon, youth-skewed tone of voice. Leans into honesty about insurance being boring and confusing, rather than pretending otherwise.

Few competitors in the Australian motor category credibly hold all three positions at once.
