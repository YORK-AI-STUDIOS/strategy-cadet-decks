Part 05 · Competitive Context · GEO recommendations

Three moves to lift GEO presence. Own the citation, do not rent it from Canstar and Finder.

From the GEO extension scan
Probable
ROLLiN’s PRIMARY positions today are earned through third-party editorial. AI models cite Canstar and Finder, not rollininsurance.com.au. That makes the GEO position fragile: an editorial change at Canstar can erase it overnight. Each recommendation moves a citable fact onto brand-owned content, or claims an uncontested query gap.
Recommendation01

Publish a dedicated “No age-based excess” explainer.

ROLLiN’ owns the no-age-excess differentiator, but it lives in review-site summaries rather than branded content an AI model can cite directly. A 500-word article, “Why ROLLiN’ doesn’t charge young drivers an age excess”, would give AI models a direct-citation source. Pitch it to Finder and Canstar for inbound links.

Targets
Q3Cheapest car insurance, under-25 drivers
Expected lift: Probable. Could move Q3 from Visible-Primary to a consistently cited top answer.
Recommendation02

Publish a “month-to-month, no exit fee” landing page.

Q5 is already Visible-Primary because Canstar quotes the feature explicitly, but the citation traces to Canstar, not to ROLLiN’. A brand-owned page ranked for “flexible car insurance Australia” or “no lock-in car insurance Australia” would let AI models cite ROLLiN’ directly, de-risking the position from Canstar editorial changes.

Targets
Q5No lock-in, flexible monthly car insurance
Effect: de-risks an existing Primary position from intermediary dependence. Seek inbound links from Canstar and Mozo.
Recommendation03

Target the “best car insurance app” content gap.

Q2 and Q6 show no single insurer dominates “best car insurance app” queries, an uncontested GEO position. ROLLiN’ is described as digital-first but has no indexed content benchmarking its app. A comparison post or a PR push around app ratings and features would seed a new AI-citable asset in a gap that currently has no winner.

Targets
Q2Q6Best app-based car insurance
Opportunity: an uncontested query with no category winner. First credible asset could claim it.
