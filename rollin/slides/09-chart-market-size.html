What this means
ROLLiN’ competes in the AUD 22.6B comprehensive-motor sub-segment, not the AUD 32.7B broad number.

The narrower scope is the one that matters for the deck. Excluding CTP and commercial motor sharpens the competitive frame to consumer-direct comprehensive, where every benchmark in this report is drawn.

  • 6.34% CAGR rewards the price-led entrant disproportionately.
  • Telematics-adjacent market (USD 6.9B by 2031) gives ROLLiN’ Safe ’n Save a coherent secondary play.
