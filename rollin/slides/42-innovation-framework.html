Ten innovation types, one structured idea grid. ROLLiN’ over-indexes on experience, under-indexes on configuration.

Method · Deloitte Ten Types
Probable
Every innovation idea in this section is generated against Deloitte’s Ten Types of Innovation, the standard discipline for moving past product-only thinking. The ten types fall into three groups, Configuration (how the business is built and earns), Offering (the product itself), and Experience (how customers find, buy and feel the brand). Read against the .md idea bank, ROLLiN’s opportunity is unevenly distributed: deep on Experience and Offering, thin on Configuration.
