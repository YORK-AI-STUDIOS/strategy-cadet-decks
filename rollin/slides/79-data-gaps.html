What this report cannot claim, and what would close each gap.

Disclosing the limits of the evidence is part of the method, not an apology for it. Each gap below names exactly what could not be verified, and the costed upgrade path that would close it. The methodology is built to be upgraded.

Data extensions · method limits
GEO is a web-search proxyUncertain

The AI-visibility scan uses web search as a proxy for direct AI-model querying. Results may lag actual model behaviour by months.

To closeDirect paid-API access to the major frontier AI models, queried on a fixed prompt panel.
Share of search is relative, not absoluteProbable

Google Trends reports relative interest, not search volume, and is sampled. ROLLiN' at 0.8% sits near the floor of reliable measurement.

To closeA paid search-volume subscription converts relative interest into absolute query counts.
Meta Ads Library shows active ads onlyProbable

Recently ended campaigns are excluded. Programmatic, display, SEM and OOH are not indexed, and AU TikTok paid activity is invisible to public tools.

To closeA licensed competitive-ad-intelligence platform indexes ended campaigns and cross-channel spend.
ProductReview sample is thinUncertain

Nine reviews is a small base for theme extraction. The platform over-represents 1-star and 5-star ratings, so the 78% negative rate is a dissatisfaction signal, not a CSAT score.

To closeA commissioned AU consumer survey, n=400 or more, for a robust satisfaction read.
Community sentiment is missingUncertain

Finder's 28 reviews sit behind a JavaScript wall. Reddit, Mozo, Trustpilot and Whirlpool were blocked or unavailable. AU community conversation is a real gap.

To closeA licensed social-listening platform such as Brandwatch or Meltwater captures forum conversation at scale.
Social engagement could not be calculatedUncertain

Platform scraping was blocked across Instagram, Facebook, TikTok and YouTube. Follower counts come from search-index metadata that lags by days.

To closeA paid social-analytics API returns verified engagement rates across every platform.
