The idea bank converges into five business-planning concepts, each landed three ways.

Method · Concept x Audience
Probable
Twenty-plus innovation ideas per audience are too many to act on. The business-planning step consolidates them into five named concepts, each one a portfolio that bundles related ideas into a single coherent move. Every concept is then tailored to all three audiences with its own go-to-market, channel mix and success metric. The matrix that follows is five concepts down, three audiences across.
01
The Fair-Go Fairness Stack
A consolidated, audited public charter of pricing-fairness commitments. No price walking, no age-loaded excess gouging, no surprise exclusions. The defensible brand moat.
02
The Multi-Modal Mobility Membership
A single-policy, single-bill subscription spanning car, car-share, e-bike, e-scooter and rideshare-as-passenger. Repositions ROLLiN’ from motor insurer to urban mobility insurer.
03
The Driver Data Wallet & Portability Promise
A customer-owned telematics ledger, visible, exportable and portable to any future insurer. Breaks the data-asymmetry lock-in that defines the category.
04
Conversational AI Claims & Service Layer
A vision-led claims experience that replaces the 40-minute phone claim with a four-minute conversational flow. Human escalation stays visible, fast and specialised.
05
Climate-Honest Cover & Resilience Concierge
Pre-quote climate transparency plus a post-purchase resilience advisory service. Positions ROLLiN’ as the climate-honest insurer, not the climate-walks-away insurer.
Step 01 · Cluster
Ideas group into concepts
Related innovation ideas are bundled so they launch as one connected portfolio, not as scattered features competing for the same budget line.
Step 02 · Tailor
Each concept is landed per audience
A concept is sold differently to each cohort: the same Fairness Stack reads as an excess reset for Gen Z, a renewal receipt for renters, a claim-denial pledge for gig drivers.
Step 03 · Channel & measure
Investment follows the proof point
Each audience cut carries its own channel mix and a single hero metric, so spend is sequenced behind the move most likely to convert that cohort first.
