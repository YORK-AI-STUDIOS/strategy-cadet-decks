Read across
Every trigger is an operating-cost or risk event.

This audience does not browse insurance, it reacts to business events: a platform change, a tax deadline, a slow month, a near-miss. ROLLiN’s monthly billing and no-penalty pause answer the income-volatility triggers directly. Commercial-use clarity is the unmet need behind point one.
