What this means
A 20-point demand-capture deficit against the value-segment leader.

Budget Direct’s 21.5% share of search is structural, the product of 19 consecutive Canstar national awards plus consistent above-the-line spend. ROLLiN’s 0.8% is not a positioning failure, it is an awareness failure.

  • Awards parity already achieved (Canstar, Mozo, Finder 2025–26).
  • Voice gap is the binding constraint, not preference.
  • Risk: Bingle’s “No Fluffy Bits” relaunch may compress this window inside twelve months.
