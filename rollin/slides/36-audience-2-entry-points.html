Read across
Renewal and admin are the dominant doorways.

This cohort enters the category on occasions, the renewal envelope, a new car, the Sunday admin block, rather than emotion. Comparison sites and aggregator articles sit between trigger and decision. Award-stacking and a frictionless mobile setup convert these moments.
