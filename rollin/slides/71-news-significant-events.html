Activity rhythm

Two campaign waves, an H2 partnership cluster, awards every November.

  • Two major campaign waves in 12 months: Mar 2025 brand platform, Jan 2026 product flight. Cadence aligns with start-of-year, back-to-uni purchase-intent windows.
  • Partnerships cluster in H2 2025: RAVIN AI in Oct, The Daily Aus in Sep, JOLT from late 2024 into summer. H2 is the brand’s commercial-partnership push.
  • Awards land Nov to Dec each year. Canstar and Mozo are evergreen anchors converted into paid acquisition proof points.
  • No Q3 2025 activity surfaced. July to August is likely a quiet window.
The through-line

Telematics is no longer a feature.

Safe ‘n Save launched as a product in late 2023. By 2025 it carries a full national campaign, anchors the JOLT partnership, and its 75% discount-uptake figure is used as proof of concept. It is becoming the brand’s primary point of differentiation.
