Theme 2: Backed by the biggest, built like the smallest.

Brand DNA · Messaging theme 2 of 3
Probable
Hook
ROLLiN’ looks and feels like a startup but is underwritten and claims-handled by IAG. The strategic reassurance theme.
This theme exists to neutralise the “digital insurer = thin balance sheet” objection before it becomes a purchase blocker. The IAG relationship is the deck’s single biggest under-leveraged asset. Most prospects do not know ROLLiN’ is IAG, and the brand has been quiet about it. Bringing the connection forward is low-cost, high-trust.
01Leadership content

Founders or leadership openly explaining the IAG relationship.

Short-form interview content where ROLLiN’ leadership describes the IAG-backed structure in plain language. Framed as the safety net behind the simple app, not as corporate parentage.

Closes the credibility gap that thinner digital plays cannot close.

Channel · LinkedIn · YouTube · PR
02Claims proof

Claims testimonial content with IAG-backed scale named explicitly.

Real customers describing how fast and uncomplicated a real claim was, with timelines and outcomes visible. The IAG infrastructure is named, not implied.

Directly addresses the 1.7-star ProductReview narrative by surfacing the cases that actually went well, with the institutional weight behind them.

Channel · Social · Owned video · Web
03Comparison

Comparison content vs. legacy insurers and unbacked digital plays.

Owned and partner content positioning ROLLiN’ against both ends of the market. Legacy insurers carry the trust but not the experience. Digital-only challengers carry the experience but not the trust.

ROLLiN’ sits between them on purpose. Make the position visible in head-to-head terms.
