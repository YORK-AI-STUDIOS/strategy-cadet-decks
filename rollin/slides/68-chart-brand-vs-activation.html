What this means
Activation captures the demand brand-building creates. ROLLiN’ is harvesting a crop it isn’t planting.

The 60% activation tilt amplifies near-term acquisition cost (price-comparison fees, performance-search bids) but does not build mental availability at the rate Binet and Field find optimal. The brand-vs-activation reset is the prerequisite for the share-of-voice play in Slide 09.

  • Suggested 12-mo glide path: 40 → 50 → 60 brand.
