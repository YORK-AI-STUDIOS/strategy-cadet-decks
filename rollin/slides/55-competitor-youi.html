Competitor 03

Youi The challenger that prices on individual driving profile, and Australia’s number one insurance advertiser.

OUTsurance Holdings (RMI)
Verified
The brand

A challenger spending well ahead of its market share.

Youi is a South African-owned challenger (OUTsurance Holdings, part of Rand Merchant Insurance Holdings). It holds approximately 5% car insurance market share in Australia and positions on personalised pricing built around an individual driving profile.

Despite that modest share, Youi is the top insurance advertiser in Australia by Nielsen ad spend (September 2024 to August 2025). It won the Finder 2026 Best Features Car Insurance award, giving creative currency to justify the spend.

Why it matters to ROLLiN’

Youi is the clearest expansion-mode brand in the set, investing media weight well in excess of share to push for structural gains. A challenger spending this hard sets the voice benchmark ROLLiN’ is measured against.
