Suncorp Group
Verified
The brand

The mass-market incumbent investing to match digital challengers.

AAMI is Suncorp Group’s largest consumer insurance brand. It has national reach, a strong TV and mass-media presence, and mid-market positioning carried by the flagship “Lucky You” creative platform.

AAMI targets a broader demographic than ROLLiN’ but competes on value and brand recognition. In January 2024 it launched an updated digital platform for mobile-first quote and purchase.

Why it matters to ROLLiN’

AAMI is in Defense Mode, protecting share against challenger pricing and digital-native entrants including ROLLiN’ while investing in digital capability to close the experience gap. Its scale means any digital improvement narrows ROLLiN’s edge.
