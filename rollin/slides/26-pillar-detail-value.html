Worth Staying For, Year After Year.

Make renewal feel like a non-event.

ROLLiN’ protects margin and lifetime value by removing the loyalty tax, ending surprise hikes, and adding small ongoing benefits that compound the longer a customer stays.

The “premium doubled while car halved” review pattern is the explicit failure mode this pillar exists to prevent. Q3 is the recommended start window because Pillars 01 and 02 need to land first. Without trust and operational delivery, a loyalty-tax-free promise reads as marketing, not behaviour.

What this looks like in executionFour tactical moves, sequenced for the second half of the year
EX 01

Loyalty-tax removal mechanic.

Engineer the renewal price so existing customers are never priced higher than new-customer equivalents. Operational, not promotional.

EX 02

Surprise-hike audit, before-and-after.

Every renewal explained: what changed, why, and what the customer’s options are. One screen. No phone call required.

EX 03

Small ongoing benefits, stacked.

Modest perks that compound with tenure. Free windshield, app-based roadside add-ons, low-friction extras that read as appreciation, not points.

EX 04

Single-tap renewal continuation.

The opposite of the renewal-letter ordeal. One-screen confirmation, full transparency, no friction. Position internally as the retention engine.
