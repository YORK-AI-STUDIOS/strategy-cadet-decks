Plan read

This cohort is the most commercially fertile, so the plan optimises for retention and advocacy, not first acquisition. The Mobility Membership is the hero, the renewal receipt is the loyalty anchor, and metrics shift from quote volume to 24-month NPS and referral. Convenience-driven renters reward transparency by staying and recommending.
