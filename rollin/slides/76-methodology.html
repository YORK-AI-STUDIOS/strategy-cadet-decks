Integrity check, nine criteria

Every synthesis claim carries a confidence chip. Every chip is auditable to its source.

Before assembly, the report runs through a nine-point integrity pass. Claims that fail any check are demoted from Verified to Probable or Uncertain, and the failure is documented inline in the citation index.

01Primary-source presence (URL accessible, not paywalled-only).
02Two-source agreement on quantitative figures.
03Date stamp on every cited dataset.
04Methodology disclosed by source publisher.
05No LLM-only claim without “Probable” chip.
06No claim about private financials of subsidiaries.
07Citation index covers 100% of synthesis claims.
08Data gaps explicit, not glossed.
09Confidence chip distribution disclosed on this page.
