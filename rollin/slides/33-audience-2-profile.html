Audience 2, App-Native Millennial Urban Renters. Aged 26 to 35, convenience-driven, time-poor.

Profile
Verified
Core age band
26 – 35
Large 25 to 34 cluster, the single largest age cohort in Australia per ABS 2021 Census.
The most commercially fertile audience for ROLLiN’. They have the income to afford a credible product, the digital literacy to fully use the app, the subscription mindset to embrace monthly billing without friction, and a renewal-driven switching habit that opens clear acquisition windows. The emotional purchase driver is not fear but convenience and control, they want insurance that runs itself and stays out of their way.

Lifestyle and values
Subscription-mindset: manages Netflix, Spotify, gym, meal kits all on monthly cycles. Insurance as a subscription makes intuitive sense. Price-aware but not price-dominated, will pay a reasonable premium for quality and convenience, but compares at renewal. Trusts aggregator-vetted awards over brand advertising. Open to telematics if privacy is respected and the value proposition is clear.
  • Renewal-driven switching is habitual. 42% compare auto insurance annually. The renewal envelope is the primary consideration trigger.
  • IAG parentage is a credibility anchor for this cohort, an institutional layer pure insurtechs lack. They choose best value, where value includes brand credibility, digital experience, and claims certainty.
