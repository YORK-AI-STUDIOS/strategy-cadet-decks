Competitor 06

Coles Insurance A supermarket-distributed value brand. Strong product, but no dedicated app and a narrower digital relationship.

Auto & General
Verified
The brand

Distribution as the strategy. The supermarket flywheel, not the app.

Coles Insurance is underwritten by Auto and General. It leverages the Coles supermarket ecosystem and loyalty flywheel for distribution, positioning on simplicity, Flybuys integration, and first-year online discounts.

The product is well regarded. It won the Finder Best Features Car Insurance and Best Low Cost Car Insurance 2026 awards. But reviewers flag a competitive gap: it has no dedicated insurance app.

Why it matters to ROLLiN’

Coles competes with ROLLiN’ on digital-first purchase and value messaging, but targets an older, grocery-habituated demographic rather than young drivers. The missing app constrains the engaged digital relationship ROLLiN’ builds.
