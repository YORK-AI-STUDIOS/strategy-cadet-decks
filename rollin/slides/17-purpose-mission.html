Purpose lifts the floor. Mission delivers it.

Brand DNA · Purpose & mission
Purpose, beyond profitP

Make insurance feel proportionate to its actual role in a person’s life.

A sensible background utility, not a yearly ritual of confusion and anxiety.

The deeper purpose is to lift the floor on how Australian drivers, especially younger ones, experience financial protection. If insurance is easier to understand, easier to buy, and easier to claim on, more people end up adequately covered.

The brand is, in effect, IAG’s experiment in whether kindness, clarity, and self-service can do more for protection rates than scale and advertising spend alone.

Mission, paraphrasedM

Simple, honest, app-based car insurance for everyday Australian drivers.

No jargon, no upsell mazes, no surprises at claim time.

ROLLiN’s mission, as expressed through its public positioning, is to deliver simple, honest, app-based car insurance for everyday Australian drivers, with no jargon, no upsell mazes, and no surprises at claim time.

A verbatim public mission statement is not reliably retrievable from training data, so this is paraphrased from consistent brand language rather than quoted directly.
