What this means
Zero broadcast means zero reach above niche-digital.

The category spends 38% of media in TV and BVOD. ROLLiN’ spends nothing. That single line explains the share-of-search gap better than any other variable.

  • JOLT EV-charging OOH partnership accounts for ~14pp of the OOH share.
  • Re-baselining to 20% TV / 10% BVOD would cost ~AUD 7M / year.
  • Funded by reallocation, this is the single intervention with highest expected SoS lift.
