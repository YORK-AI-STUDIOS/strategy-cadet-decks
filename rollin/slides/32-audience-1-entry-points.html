Read across
Four of seven triggers are emotional or need-state.

Life-stage moments open the category. Emotion-led moments, renewal shock and unfair pricing, are where ROLLiN’s fairness positioning converts. The app store and budget-review triggers reward always-on digital presence over campaign bursts.
