Bingle The lowest-cost, no-frills online insurer. ROLLiN’s closest rival in the price-sensitive, digital-native segment.

Suncorp Group
Verified
The brand

An online-only insurer built to compete on price alone.

Bingle is an online-only insurer owned by Suncorp Group, launched in 2007. It positions as the lowest-cost, no-frills comprehensive option, and in mid-2025 gave that position a creative platform with the “No Fluffy Bits” campaign.

The brand carries genuine product credentials. It won the Canstar Outstanding Value National Award 2025 and the Finder Best Low Cost 2025 award, the same value territory ROLLiN’ competes in.

Why it matters to ROLLiN’

Bingle is the key direct rival to ROLLiN’ in the price-sensitive, digital-native segment. The two brands chase the same value-led customer through the same digital channels, and Bingle’s 2025 above-the-line push signals it intends to defend that ground louder.
