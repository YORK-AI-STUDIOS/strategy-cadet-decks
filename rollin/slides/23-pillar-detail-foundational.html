Pillar 01: Insurance Without The Runaround.

Foundational pillar · Synthesis
Verified
01Foundational

Insurance Without The Runaround.

Every customer interaction must justify itself in seconds.

ROLLiN’ defaults to self-service, plain English, and one clear path rather than a maze of options. The flat $800 excess, no age-loading, and monthly no-lock-in structure are the operational expressions of this pillar.

The 78% claims-handling complaint rate is the gap between promise and delivery. Closing it is the first job of the next twelve months. Everything else in the strategy assumes this is being fixed in parallel.

Load-bearing numbers
2.4 ★
Independent vs aggregator gap. ProductReview 1.7 vs Finder 4.1, the chasm that defines the pillar.
78%
Claims-handling complaint rate. 7 of 9 ProductReview entries reference claims as the core issue.
$800
Flat excess, no age load. Operational expression of one clear path, no maze.
What this looks like in executionFour tactical moves, sequenced for the next twelve months
EX 01

Plain-English commitment, made public.

Lock the plain-language standard into a testable public commitment. Every customer-facing artefact measured against it.

EX 02

Single-path claims flow.

One claim journey, app-led, with status visible at every step. No re-keying, no transfers, no PDS scavenger hunts.

EX 03

Two-minute quote-to-bind.

Hold quote-to-bind under two minutes for the standard cohort. Public benchmark. Public failure rate.

EX 04

Claims SLA, published monthly.

Average first-response, time-to-decision, time-to-payment, published as a dashboard, not as marketing.
