Founding story

ROLLiN’ was launched by IAG in October 2021 as a digital-native motor insurance brand aimed at younger Australians who found traditional insurers slow, confusing, and joyless. An earlier exploratory version, Poncho, preceded it internally at IAG.

It was built to test whether a stripped-back, mobile-first, transparent product could win a generation already skipping insurance or under-insuring by default.

ROLLiN’ was incubated inside IAG as a deliberate challenger play, built by people frustrated with how opaque, paperwork-heavy, and disengaged legacy motor insurance had become.
