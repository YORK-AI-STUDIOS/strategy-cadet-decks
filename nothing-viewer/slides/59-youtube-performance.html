Content Pattern (Strategic Read)
7 of the top 10 videos are "CEO reacts to reviewer" or "CEO made your dream phone" formats. Nothing's YouTube strategy is built around earned media amplification and founder visibility. This is unusual for a consumer electronics brand. It may be a category-disruptive differentiator (community brand vs corporate brand) or it may work against a premium prestige positioning. Worth raising with the client: does this YouTube posture reflect a deliberate brand relationship strategy, or is it organic founder behaviour that has happened to perform? PROBABLE strategic read
