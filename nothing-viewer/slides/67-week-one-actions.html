Five decisions and five creative starts in week one. None require budget approval, product changes, or external dependencies. All five decisions enable the Tier 1 initiatives to ship within 30 days. The first Google Trends baseline read happens on day 7. [PROBABLE]

Strategic Synthesis · Week-one actions
Probable
Decisions (Need an Owner by Day 2)
D1
Nominate the Nothing AU Community Manager
One person, internal or agency, who owns the r/NothingTech AU verified presence, the weekly AMA cadence, and the Monday KPI review. This is the single highest-leverage hire or assignment of week one: nothing in the Tier 1 plan ships without it.
Owner: Nothing AU Strategy Lead
D2
Approve the "honest comparison including limitations" tone-of-voice policy
The Competitor Switcher Guide (Tier 1) must acknowledge what Nothing does worse than Pixel for some buyers. Legal and comms sign-off on this tone policy is the only gating dependency for the guide. Without it, the guide defaults to a standard marketing brochure that Audience 2 will dismiss.
Owner: Nothing AU Marketing Lead + Legal
D3
Establish the AU DTC revenue and conversion baselines
Kill-Switch 3 (negative AU DTC growth) and the Pixel Defector Conversion Rate KPI both require an internal baseline established in month 1. Request Nothing AU internal DTC order volume and post-purchase survey deployment at DTC checkout. Without a baseline, the KPI tree is directional only.
Owner: Nothing AU Data/Analytics Lead
D4
Confirm CMF AU distribution status (the Day-60 gate)
Not a week-one deliverable, but the Day-60 gate requires a go/no-go on CMF AU distribution. Start the internal conversation now. If the decision is not in the marketing team's control, establish who owns it and when a confirmed answer is expected.
Owner: Nothing AU Commercial Lead
D5
Set the weekly cadence start date (Monday review)
The operating cadence starts in week two. The Monday 30-minute review is the forcing function that keeps KPI drift from becoming strategy drift. Confirm the standing meeting and invite list by day 5.
Owner: Nothing AU Strategy Lead
Creative Starts (Can Begin Day 1)
C1
Draft brief: Competitor Switcher Guide (Pixel edition)
A 2,000-3,000 word SEO-optimised guide to switching from Pixel to Nothing. Structural requirement: the guide must acknowledge at least two things Nothing does worse or differently from Pixel (per tone-of-voice policy D2). Peer-review by r/NothingTech AU community before publish.
Owner: Nothing AU Content Lead
C2
Draft brief: AU YouTube tech reviewer priority access list
Identify 5-8 AU tech YouTubers with 10K+ subscribers and confirmed Pixel-owning audience signals. Prioritise channels that have already reviewed Nothing or Pixel competitors. Briefing session with the Nothing hardware team as the primary value offer, not monetary payment.
Owner: Nothing AU PR/Partnerships Lead
C3
Run a fresh Google Trends pull as the baseline read
This is the week-one data action. Pull the current 12-month rolling AU Share of Search for Nothing Phone, Google Pixel, Samsung Galaxy A, Oppo, and CMF. This is the baseline against which all Kill-Switch thresholds and KPI targets are measured. Nothing currently sits at approx. 5/100 on the normalised Trends score. Pixel at 15.9%. PROBABLE baseline from cross-platform data
Owner: Nothing AU Data/Analytics Lead
C4
Request JB Hi-Fi BNPL copy update (Afterpay/Zip at listing level)
Contact the JB Hi-Fi digital merchandising team to confirm Afterpay/Zip is displayed at product hero level on all Nothing and CMF device listings. If not live, submit the copy update request in week one. This is the lowest-effort, highest-conversion-impact action in the Tier 2 plan and should not wait 31 days to start.
Owner: Nothing AU Retail/Distribution Lead
C5
Verify Nothing Technology listing on ProductReview.com.au
Manual check: does Nothing Phone (2a) or Phone (3) have a listing on ProductReview.com.au? If absent, create a listing and flag to Nothing AU. This takes under 30 minutes and closes a trust-signal gap for Audience 3 at the consideration-to-conversion stage.
Owner: Nothing AU Community Manager (once nominated via D1)
