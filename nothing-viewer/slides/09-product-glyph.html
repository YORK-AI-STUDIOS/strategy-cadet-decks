Core product (under 100 words)

Hero products: Phone (1) through Phone (3) and Phone (4a) series; Ear and Ear (a) TWS earbuds; Headphone (1) over-ear. What makes Nothing unique

Competitors treat internals as something to hide; Nothing treats them as something to celebrate. Pricing sits below Apple and Samsung flagships while above purely value-tier Chinese imports, creating a "considered alternative" lane. The Charli XCX brand partnership in 2026 signals fashion and music credibility alongside hardware credibility. [251] Probable

Glyph Interface: the structural innovation

The Glyph Interface is Nothing's most structurally innovative concept: it turns the phone's exterior into a soft notification layer, reducing screen-on time and training users toward a calmer relationship with their device. The approach inverts the industry default, which rewards maximising screen engagement. [214] Probable

Glyph in 2025-2026: Glyph Matrix

Phone (3) introduced the Glyph Matrix, a circular dot-matrix LED array replacing the prior linear Glyph strips. This upgrade shifts the Glyph from a notification signal into a display-capable surface: the Matrix can render shapes, text, and animated visualisations. Combined with the Essential Space AI feature (converts screenshots and voice notes into structured tasks, synced to Google Calendar), Phone (3) is the first Nothing device where software-plus-hardware intelligence converge as a unified platform argument.
