Operating Cadence
Weekly Review (Every Monday)
Attendees: Nothing AU Strategy Lead and Marketing Lead. Duration: 30 minutes maximum. Review: weekly content output count, r/NothingTech AU engagement volume, AU tech press placements from prior week. Any metric more than 30% below weekly target triggers a same-week escalation to the creative brief, not a wait for monthly review.
Monthly Review (First Wednesday)
Full AU team reviews rolling 4-week KPI snapshot: AU Search Interest Index trend, Pixel Defector Conversion Rate from post-purchase surveys, Earbuds-to-Phone upgrade conversion rate. If any leading indicator is below target for two consecutive weeks before the monthly review, the session opens with a root-cause discussion. The KPI tree drives the agenda, not the calendar.
Monthly Distribution Check
At monthly review: confirm CMF AU retail placement status and JB Hi-Fi BNPL copy is live at all digital touchpoints. If either is unconfirmed, escalate to distribution lead as P1 blocker, not a planning note.
Quarterly Review (Months 3 and 6)
Goal itself is revisited. Sanity-check rerun against then-current SoS data (fresh Google Trends pull sufficient). If Oppo 3-month SoS exceeds 17%, Alternative Goal 2 (Oppo defensive) is promoted to co-primary. At 180-day mark: goal is retired or extended based on AU Search Interest Index 12-month rolling average.
Kill-Switches
Kill-Switch 1
If AU Search Index remains below 6 by day 90
Pause all Tier 2 initiatives except BNPL visibility. Commission qualitative research: minimum 12 depth interviews with AU Pixel owners aged 25-38. Restart only after insight returned. A score of 6 from a baseline of 5 is a 20% lift; failure to reach it signals mechanism failure, not goal failure. PROBABLE
Kill-Switch 2
If Oppo 3-month AU SoS exceeds 17% at month 3 or 6
Immediately add Oppo defensive creative brief to Tier 1 running brief. Head-to-head design comparison content targeting Anti-Mainstream Gadget Upgraders pulled by Oppo brand-building momentum. Do not wait for the next planning cycle. Oppo acceleration from 14.3% to 15.5% in 3 months already flagged. VERIFIED Google Trends
Kill-Switch 3
If AU DTC Revenue Growth (6-month rolling) is negative at month 6
Halt all brand-building initiatives (Design Residency, TikTok content). Redirect budget to performance-led acquisition (Google Search, Meta retargeting against Pixel-owning audiences). Brand-building without a conversion floor is a runway risk at Nothing's AU scale. No AU DTC baseline currently available; establish in month 1. HYPOTHESISED
Kill-Switch 4
If CMF AU Market Entry not confirmed by day 60
Remove CMF from 31-90 day roadmap. Redirect Gen Z acquisition to Nothing Phone (2a) at AUD 499 with aggressive BNPL framing. Day 60 is a hard gate, not a soft checkpoint. PROBABLE
