Strategic read for AU market
Nothing's founder visibility is a genuine competitive moat at the AUD 649 to 1,509 price tier. OnePlus, Motorola, and Oppo have no equivalent public founder persona. Samsung and Apple have institutional brand equity but no accessible founder narrative. The Pei persona works hardest in AU when amplified via AU tech press and community (r/NothingTech AU), not just global TikTok. The current content strategy does not appear to have AU-specific founder activation. This is an addressable gap. [PROBABLE]
