Tier 1
01
Glyph Content Studio: Creator Kit and Brief Programme
Structured brief programme for AU TikTok and YouTube creators (50K to 500K followers) with early-access hardware and creative briefs framing the Glyph Interface as a production asset. Brief defines the narrative; creator owns execution. Activates TikTok Unboxing Spiral and Content Creator CEPs at near-zero cost.
PROBABLE
Tier 1
02
ProductReview.com.au Listing: Create Today
Nothing Technology has no listing on Australia's primary consumer review platform. Creating a listing takes under 30 minutes, costs nothing, and closes an active GEO gap. The single highest-leverage, zero-cost AU action in this entire report. Every day without it is a missed trust signal for Audience 3 at the consideration-to-conversion stage.
PROBABLE
Tier 1
03
Earn Your Early Access: Community Beta Commerce
Formalise Nothing's existing beta culture into a structured programme where community contributors accumulate points redeemable for early-access device purchases. Converts passive community membership into an active loyalty engine. Grounded in Community Early Access Drop CEP (Audience 2) and documented Nothing community beta programme.
PROBABLE
Tier 2
04
CMF Halo Effect: Sub-Brand Bridge Programme
Structured upgrade pathway from CMF devices to Nothing flagship with trade-in credit and community beta invitation. Converts budget-tier buyers into full-ecosystem participants over 12 to 24 months. Grounded in Earbuds Gateway CEP (Audience 1 and 3). Formalises an evidenced behaviour pattern.

Tier 2
05
Nothing Certified: AU Repair and Longevity Network
Partner with independent repair shops in AU metropolitan centres. Branded authorised repair network with published pricing and genuine parts. Closes the post-purchase trust gap (Trustpilot 2.5) and directly addresses the ecosystem gap that Apple's Genius Bar fills. Grounded in Right-to-Repair cultural signal (Audience 2).
PROBABLE
Tier 2
06
Transparent Drop: Monthly Co-Design Collab Series
Monthly collaboration with emerging AU designers, artists, and musicians to produce limited transparent-back device colourways. Community-voted. Each drop documented via behind-the-scenes content. Generates organic social content while deepening cultural authenticity. Grounded in Fragmented Fandom trend (Audience 1) and Nothing's evidenced community-first launch culture.
PROBABLE
Tier 2
07
BNPL-Native Accessories Bundle
Curated accessories bundle (case, CMF Watch, Ear buds, Glyph charging pad) at AUD 299 to 399 as a single BNPL-eligible purchase at JB Hi-Fi and DTC. Converts first-time buyers into multi-product ecosystem participants at point of sale. Grounded in BNPL Accessibility CEP (Audience 3) and documented Afterpay/Zip availability.
PROBABLE
Tier 2
08
Nothing Open: Radical Transparency Annual Report
Annual "State of Nothing" report covering materials sourcing, software support commitments, repair rates, community contributions, and honest assessment of what did not work. Distributed via community forum, pitched to design and tech press. Positions Nothing as the industry's most candid OEM disclosure. Grounded in Brand DNA Theme 3 and transparency brand value.
