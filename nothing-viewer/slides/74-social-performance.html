Strategist consideration (assisted, not directed)
The active paid strategy appears to assume awareness and consideration are built through organic social, not paid media. This is a defensible posture for a brand with 1.2M TikTok followers and 5.74% engagement. It also limits the ability to grow beyond the existing audience. Worth exploring whether the AU market, where Nothing has 3.6% share of search and no sustained between-launch presence, requires a paid awareness layer that the global strategy does not currently run.
