Audience 3: Budget-Conscious Gen Z Experimenters (18-27, Social-Proof Driven). Income: AUD 25,000-65,000. BNPL as normalised budget management tool [VERIFIED GWI]. Purchase drivers: Price and Cost of Ownership (5/5), Product Quality and Performance (4/5). Top CEPs: TikTok Unboxing Spiral (Social), BNPL Accessibility Moment (Emotion). Signal: Social Proof Discovery and Mobile-First Shopping are sequential stages in one continuous journey that almost never passes through a desktop browser. The gap is AU-dedicated content calibrated to price transparency, authentic comparisons, and creator-focused positioning.
Primary
TikTok (dedicated AU account or AU content stream)

Primary
Instagram Reels
Authentic Reels discovery from creators and peer accounts is a primary route for this cohort. Instagram's product tagging and DTC link features support Mobile-First Shopping signal by compressing the discovery-to-checkout journey. Social Proof Discovery signal runs across both TikTok and IG for this cohort. PROBABLE
Format: Reels 15-30 seconds, product tags with BNPL price, Story links to DTC or JB Hi-Fi.
Secondary
YouTube (creator review and comparison)
Experience-Driven Tech Upgrades signal confirms this audience validates social discovery with YouTube comparison reviews before purchasing. Content Creator Tool Upgrade CEP means they also watch creator-specific device reviews to evaluate the phone as a production tool. PROBABLE
Format: 8-15 minute honest comparison reviews, creator edition content, "best budget phone AU 2025" format.
