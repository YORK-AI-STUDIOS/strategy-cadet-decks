Key CEP findings
4
Cross-audience CEPs (universal triggers)
Anti-Apple Defection, Earbuds Gateway, Social Proof Discovery, and Spec-Sheet Validation appear across two or more audiences. Activate these first -- lowest media cost, widest reach.
2
Highest-leverage zero-cost CEPs
Reddit Rabbit Hole (Aud 2, score 5) and Earbuds Gateway (Aud 3, score 5). Both community-driven, both directly addressable without paid media. Priority for Tier 1 activation window.
3
Audience 1 top CEPs (score 5)
Glyph Unboxing Moment, Anti-Apple Statement Upgrade. Both are visual, identity-driven, and shareable. TikTok and Instagram are the natural distribution channels.
5
Audience 2 top CEPs (score 5)
Reddit Rabbit Hole, Spec-Sheet Validation, iPhone-to-Android Defection. All research-heavy, rational-validation triggers. Long-form content and comparison tools are the format match.
5
Audience 3 top CEPs (score 5)
TikTok Unboxing Spiral, Earbuds Gateway Entry. Both are discovery-led, impulse-informed, and BNPL-compatible. Short-form video + in-store placement at JB Hi-Fi are the activation levers.
